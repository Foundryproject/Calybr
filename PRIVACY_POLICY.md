# Privacy Policy for Calybr

**Last Updated:** October 30, 2024

## Welcome to Calybr!

At Calybr, we're committed to helping you become a safer driver while respecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## What Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **When:** While you're driving and when the app is open
- **Why:** To automatically detect trips, track your routes, and calculate your driving score
- **How:** We use GPS to record your location during trips
- **Background Access:** Yes, we need "Always Allow" location permission to automatically detect when you start driving, even when the app is closed

### Driving Behavior Data
- **What:** Speed, acceleration, braking patterns, phone usage while driving, and driving times
- **Why:** To calculate your personalized driving score and provide safety tips
- **When:** Only during detected trips

### Account Information
- **What:** Name, email address, phone number (optional), vehicle details
- **Why:** To create your account and personalize your experience
- **How:** Collected when you sign up via email or Google

### Device Information
- **What:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Why:** To ensure the app works properly and fix technical issues

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Features
- **Trip Detection:** Automatically detect when you start and stop driving
- **Driving Score:** Calculate your personalized safety score based on your driving behavior
- **Route Tracking:** Show you where you've driven and provide trip summaries
- **Safety Insights:** Provide tips to help you improve your driving

### Optional Features
- **Leaderboards:** Compare your score with friends (only if you choose to participate)
- **Rewards:** Qualify for rewards or insurance discounts based on safe driving
- **Community:** Share achievements with other Calybr users

### Improvements
- **App Enhancement:** Analyze anonymized data to improve our scoring algorithm and features
- **Customer Support:** Help you with technical issues or questions

## Location Permissions Explained

### Why We Need "Always Allow"
Calybr needs background location access to:
1. **Automatically detect** when you start driving (hands-free experience)
2. **Record complete trips** without you having to open the app
3. **Calculate accurate scores** based on your entire trip

### Your Control
- You can disable automatic trip detection in Settings
- Location tracking only happens during trips (not 24/7)
- You can view all recorded trips and delete any you don't want

### Battery Impact
We've optimized location tracking to minimize battery usage, but background tracking does use some battery. Most users see minimal impact.

## State-Specific Privacy Rights

### California Residents (CCPA)
If you're a California resident, you have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information we collect
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (Note: We don't sell your data)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@calybr.app
